The Challenge

2025–2026 UNEARTHED Season

This year's FIRST LEGO League challenge is all about archaeology and underground exploration — uncovering the past to discover the future.

⛏

15 Robot Missions

From surface brushing and map reveals to statue rebuilds and salvage operations — all completed in 2.5-minute rounds with precision tokens.

💡

Innovation Project

Teams research how to discover, preserve, and learn from history — or how tools from the past can solve today's challenges. Judged on research, teamwork, and prototyping.

🌐

APOC 2026 — Sydney

60 invitation-only teams from across the globe compete at Macquarie University, July 9–12. Free spectator entry. Bonus workshop day on July 13.

🚀

Future Edition Coming

From 2026–27, FLL transitions to a new "Future Edition" with AI-enabled sensors and a new kit — running in parallel with the current SPIKE-based system until 2028–29.
